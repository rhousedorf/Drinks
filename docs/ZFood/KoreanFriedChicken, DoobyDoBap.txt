Wet Brine 
for every 1kg of chicken + water
30g Master Curing Blend
15g Seasoning Blend 30g white vinegar 

Dredge 900g AP Flour 
100g Tapioca Flour 
30g Master Curing Blend 
10g Spice Blend
If only for 1 chicken, use 180g Flour 20g Tapioca Flour 1.5g Cure Blend, 2g 20g Spice Blend  (I said 10g in the video but later on upped it & found it better)

Shake off excess marinade 
Drop into dredge mix 
Agitate for 20-30 times
Dunk in cold water for 10-20 seconds 
Let excess water drip off 
Dunk again into dredge mixAgitate for 10-20 times

Fry at 165C for first fry 6-7 minutes or until golden brown Let it rest for 30 minutes at least Fry again at 195C for 1-2 minutes
