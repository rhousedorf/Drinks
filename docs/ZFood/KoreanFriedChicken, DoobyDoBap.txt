Step 1 - Prep chicken.

Step 2
 Chicken  & add water to 1000g 

Step3
 Add 30g Master Cure
 Add 15 g Seasoning 
  ( NO SALT IN THE SEASONING!!!) 
  or else use 20g Cure

Step 4
 Add 30g distilled vinegar

Step 5
 Marinate 1 hr min, 
 4 hr to overnite

Step 6
 4 pieces at a time
 Shake off excess marinade 
 Drop into dredge mix 
 Agitate for 20-30 times
 leave it in the flour for 30 sec MIN.

 Dunk in cold water for 5 seconds 
 until bubles stop?
 USE A WIRE BASKET
 Let excess water drip off 
 Dunk again into dredge mix
 Agitate for 10-20 times
 LEAVE it again, 30 sec

Fry at 180C,370F  for first fry 6-7 minutes
temp will drop to 165C,330F
 or until golden brown 
 
Let it rest for 30 minutes at least 

Fry again at 195C, 390F for 1-2 minutes
