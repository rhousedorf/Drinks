
Lighter Drinks



THE CHARLIE CHAPLIN RECIPE 0:51
1 oz. (30 ml) Plymouth Sloe Gin
1 oz. (30 ml) Giffard Abricot du Roussillon
1 oz. (30 ml) fresh lime juice
Dehydrated lime wheel for garnish

THE CHRYSANTHEMUM RECIPE 2:57
1.5 oz. (45 ml) Dolin (White) Vermouth de Chambéry Dry
3/4 oz. (22.5 ml) Bénédictine D.O.M.
3 dashes St. George Absinthe Verte
1 swath orange peel
Expressed orange oil and peel for garnish

THE BITTER GIUSEPPE RECIPE 4:50
2 oz. (60 ml) Cynar
1 oz. (30 ml) Cocchi Vermouth di Tornino
1/4 oz. (7.5 ml) fresh lemon juice
4 dashes Regan’s Orange Bitters No. 6
1 swath lemon peel
Expressed lemon oil and peel for garnish
