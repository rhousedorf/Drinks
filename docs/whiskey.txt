Whiskey Sour:
Build in shaker. 
Add
1 oz. or 30 ml. Lemon Juice
1 oz. or 30 ml. Simple Syrup
2 oz. or 60 ml. Bourbon
1 Egg White
Dry shake
Add ice and shake again
Strain into glass
Garnish with 4 drops of Angostura Bitters


THE IRISH BLACKTHORN RECIPE 6:35
1.5 oz (45 ml) Redbreast 12 year Irish Whiskey
1.5 oz (45 ml) Dolin Blanc Vermouth de Chambéry
2 dashes St. George Absinthe Verte  BE VERY CAREFUL WITH THE Absinthe.  REALLY, ONLY, 2 drops.
3 dashes Fee Bros. Cardamom (Boker’s Style) Bitters
