Sazerac:
Build in mixing glass
Add
2 oz. or 60 ml. Rye Whiskey
2 bar spoon of Simple Syrup
6 Dashes Peychaud's Bitters
Crack ice in to mixing glass and stir
Spritz glass with Absinthe
Strain into glass 
Garnish with a Twist of Lemon

Manhattan:
Build in mixing glass
Add
2 oz. or 60 ml. Rye
1 oz. or 30 ml. Sweet Vermouth
2 Dashes of Angostura Bitters
Add ice into mixing glass and stir. 
Strain into glass.
Garnish with 2 Maraschino Cherries
