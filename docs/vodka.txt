 of high-quality cold brew coffee concentrate, which provides the necessary strength and flavor. Simply shake 
 vodka, 
 coffee liqueur (like Kahlúa), and 
 cold brew with ice, then strain into a chilled glass. 

Key Alternatives & Tips
Cold Brew Concentrate: This is the best substitute for a thick, foamy, and strong cocktail.
Instant Coffee: Mix 
 of instant coffee with 
 of hot water to dissolve, then use as the coffee component.
Strong Brewed Coffee: Use a French press to make very strong, dark coffee, let it cool completely, and use 
.
Foam Guarantee: If you want a thicker foam without real espresso, add 
 of egg white or 2 tablespoons of aquafaba to the shaker.
Simple Syrup: Add 
 of simple syrup if you prefer it sweeter, though many find coffee liqueur provides enough sweetness.
